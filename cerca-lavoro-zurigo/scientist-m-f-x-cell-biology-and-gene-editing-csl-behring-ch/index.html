Scientist (m/f/x) – Cell Biology and Gene Editing

Temporary position, 1 year | Schlieren, Switzerland

We are looking for a hands-on Scientist to join our Mechanistic Pharmacology team within Translational Research in Schlieren.

  • Scientist (m/f/x) – Cell Biology and Gene Editing
  • Temporary position, 1 year | Schlieren, Switzerland
  • Master’s degree in Cell Biology, Life Sciences, or a related scientific discipline, or equivalent relevant laboratory experience; industry experience is an advantage.
  • At least 3 years of practical laboratory experience in cell biology, including strong expertise in human cell culture, such as HUVECs, and transfection methods.

Requisiti principali

  • Master’s degree in Cell Biology, Life Sciences, or a related scientific discipline, or equivalent relevant laboratory experience; industry experience is an advantage.
  • At least 3 years of practical laboratory experience in cell biology, including strong expertise in human cell culture, such as HUVECs, and transfection methods.
  • Hands-on experience with CRISPR-based gene editing approaches.
  • Proficiency in molecular biology techniques, including RT-qPCR, immunoassays and Western blotting.
  • Experience with imaging-based techniques is an advantage.
  • Experience in troubleshooting experiments, handling laboratory equipment, and optimizing workflows to improve assay robustness and performance.
  • Ability to work independently and take ownership of experiments from study design through execution, analysis, and presentation of results.

Processo di candidatura

  • Develop and apply human in vitro models and functional assay workflows to support translational research programs.

Azienda e contesto

  • Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and commitment to high-quality experimental documentation.
  • Proficiency in written and spoken English.
