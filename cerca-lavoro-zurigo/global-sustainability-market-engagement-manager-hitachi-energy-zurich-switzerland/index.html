The Opportunity The Global Sustainability Market Engagement Manager enables the organization to systematically anticipate, structure, and respond to sustainability‑related customer and stakeholder expectations .

The role supports the transition from reactive, ad‑hoc handling of sustainability requests to a proactive, structured, and insight‑driven approach , by developing shared response processes, facilitating cross‑functional alignment, and translating external market signals into strategic sustainability intelligence.

Market & Stakeholder Sustainability Intelligence Gather and structure sustainability expectations, requirements, and emerging topics from customers and key stakeholders.

Identify trends, recurring themes, and early signals relevant to competitiveness, market access, and organisational risk.

Maintain an overview of sustainability topics with increasing market and stakeholder relevance.

Sustainability Request & Response Process Development Develop and continuously improve a structured process to manage sustainability-related customer and stakeholder requests.

Define clear intake, coordination, prioritisation, escalation, and response pathways.

Enable effective collaboration across Sustainability, Marketing & Sales, Portfolio, Product Management, Supply Chain, Legal, and other functions.

Enable Consistent and Proactive Market Engagement Develop guidance, tools, and response frameworks for recurring sustainability topics.

Support a proactive approach to addressing future sustainability expectations and customer needs.
