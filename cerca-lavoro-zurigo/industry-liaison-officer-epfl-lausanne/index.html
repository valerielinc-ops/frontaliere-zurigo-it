Panoramica

The Industry Liaison Officer develops, supports and nurtures a trustful relationship between EPFL's industrial partners and EPFL ecosystem.

You will be responsible for establishing and maintaining long-term relationship between industrial accounts and EPFL.

You will be able to identify needs and requirements to develop industry – academia collaboration.

  • The Industry Liaison Officer develops, supports and nurtures a trustful relationship between EPFL's industrial partners and EPFL ecosystem.
  • You will be responsible for establishing and maintaining long-term relationship between industrial accounts and EPFL.
  • Main duties and responsibilities
  • Foster trustful relationship with a portfolio of companies having a long-term relationship with EPFL.

Responsabilità principali

  • Main duties and responsibilities
  • Foster trustful relationship with a portfolio of companies having a long-term relationship with EPFL.
  • Build relationships with new industry partners.
  • Actively contribute to the design, delivery and continuous improvement of our Open Innovation Program, ensuring a high-value experience for participating companies and alignment with institutional objectives.
  • Acquire a thorough understanding of companies and professors' needs and ensure appropriate collaboration models and services are developed and delivered.
  • Link to other EPFL entities depending on the needs.
  • Acquire a strong knowledge and understanding of EPFL ecosystem for helping companies to identify the right partners for their innovation projects
  • Be proactive in proposing new potential collaboration opportunities by following EPFL research activities.
  • Resolve any issues and problems the partner faces and deal with complaints to maintain trust and enrich existing collaboration.
  • Serve as the communication link between companies, researchers, EPFL & VPI teams and services.

Azienda e contesto

  • Excellent organizational skills and ability in problem-solving and negotiation.
  • Excellent presentation skills.
