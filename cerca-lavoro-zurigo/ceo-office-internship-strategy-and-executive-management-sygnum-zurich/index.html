The CEO Office supports Sygnum’s Board of Directors, Group CEO and Group Executive Board on strategic priorities, executive decision-making and company-wide initiatives.

As a CEO Office Intern, you will work directly with the Chief of Staff and gain broad exposure to Sygnum’s strategy, operations and senior leadership.

You will contribute to executive and Board materials, conduct strategic analyses and help drive cross-functional projects across the organisation.
