Processo di candidatura

  • Degree in Electrical/Electronics Engineering or a related technical field with at least 5 years of hands-on experience in electronics design and layout for industrial applications
  • Expert-level proficiency in at least one industry-standard ECAD software tool (e.g., Altium, KiCad, Cadence)
  • Proven track record of successfully designing complex multi-layer PCBs with strict functional safety and EMI/EMC compliance requirements
  • Deep technical knowledge of signal integrity, high-speed routing, controlled impedance, length matching, and return path optimization
  • Extensive experience navigating DFM/DFA constraints and collaborating directly with EMS partners for hardware productization
  • Dedicated mindset toward component creation, database cleanliness, and rigorous library management
  • Proactive, highly accountable professional who takes clear technical decisions and thrives in a fast-paced environment Bonus Points
  • Experience with rigid-flex PCB designs or layout techniques for wireless technologies
