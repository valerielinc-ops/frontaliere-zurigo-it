Responsabilità principali

  • In addition to architecture and delivery responsibilities, the role transitions into the on-site owner for IT run services, ensuring stable operations, vendor coordination, and continuous improvement after project completion.
  • Your tasks: Define, design, and govern network architectures within the BSF site project, aligned with global IT/OT network architectures across enterprise and industrial environments.
  • Design and implement segmented IT/OT architectures (zones, DMZs, VLANs, secure data flows) and support industrial environments including SCADA, PLC, DCS, MES, and shop-floor systems.
  • Act as primary infrastructure lead during project BSF, coordinating network and hosting activities between site management, project teams and Global IT while ensuring infrastructure readiness (network, connectivity, hosting, security) to support all project milestones and commissioning activities.
  • Drive alignment between project design and corporate standards, including architecture reviews and approvals.
  • Support site deployment activities including assessments, design validation, cutovers, testing, and go-live.
  • Coordinate integration of network and hosting platforms (on-prem, hybrid, cloud) into end-to-end infrastructure solutions & ensure systems are designed for high availability, security, scalability, and operational sustainability.
  • Govern and coordinate external service providers, system integrators, and network vendors.
  • Review designs, proposals, and delivery quality of suppliers.
  • Ensure compliance with GxP, data integrity, and cybersecurity standards (e.g., IEC 62443, ISO 27001) and support audits, risk assessments, and regulatory inspections.
